Pillars

Health

Do you exercise? Do you eat well? Sleep well? How do you take care of your health?

Spiritual

Do you believe in God, in a Higher Being? Do you seek to pray, meditate, practice your faith?

Relatives (parent and sibling relationship)

Did you grow up in a happy home? You have a relationship with your parents and brothers is it healthy?

Conjugal

Is there affection, respect and care?

Children

Do you have children? Think about having? Don't you want kids? What do you think about children?

Social

Do you have good interpersonal relationships, whether at work or in studies?

Serve

Does it help your community? Do you volunteer work? Have you been helping anyone in any way? Do you use your time, your knowledge or your donations to help someone?

Intellectual

Do you study regularly? What do you like to study or why do you have to study? What would it be like to study what you like?

Financial

Is your life financially balanced? Do you earn more than you spend or is it the other way around? Have you been worried about having a savings, reserve money, worries about saving for the future, to realize your dreams?

Professional

How do you feel about your job today? Are you happy in your area or would you like to act in something completely different? Is your salary satisfactory? Your working environment is healthy?

Emotional

How do you handle your emotions? Do you speak what you feel or keep? When speak explode or wait for the right moment? Do you have grievances? Do you believe you need to forgive or be forgiven?
